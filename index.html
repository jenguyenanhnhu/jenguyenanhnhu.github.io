Nguyễn Anh Như

I go by Jenny Nguyen or Nguyễn Anh Như. I am an early career professional who wears many hats.

I have always been curious and wanted to learn more about society, industry, and the world. As a result, I often put my hand up for anything that comes my way and frequently throw myself into the deep end. Hence, I graduated from an arts degree majoring in History and completed my Honours thesis on Vietnamese women entrepreneurs in the 17th and 18th centuries - only to jump into the tech world as a consultant. It's also led to me accomplishing some really cool and different things, including (but not limited to): being involved in Brisbane's startup community, promoting sustainability in fashion, and empowering young people to have their voice heard. Along the way, I have gained communication and collaboration skills, research and writing skills, and learnt how to be adaptable and flexible in any situation.

If you want to know more, feel free to browse through my portfolio or reach out for a chat.
